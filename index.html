FAQ

Question 1: How do I register on the platform?

Answer: To register on the platform, click the "Register" button in the top right corner and fill in all the required fields.

Question 2: How do I access the courses?

Answer: After registering and logging in, select the course you are interested in and click the "Enroll" button.

Question 3: What courses are available on the platform?

Answer: The platform offers courses for preparing for the UNT in mathematics and computer science.
